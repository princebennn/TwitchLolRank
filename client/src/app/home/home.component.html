What to do with TwitchLolRank ?

Say to other people on Twitch if you are beginner or master !

You can search twitch users to see what are there League of Legends accounts and rank.
You can link your League of Legends accounts to your Twitch account to say to other twitch users what is your rank.

How to link your Twitch and Lol accounts ?

1Go on League of legends and invite twitchlolrank as friend. You will instantly receive a link by chat.
2Go on the link and click on the claim button. You will be able to link to the Twitch account you want
3It's all right you have succesfully bind your twitch and League of legends accounts. You can register more League of legends account to your Twitch account or link your League of Legends account to another Twitch account, just repeat the same process.
